Full Rankings

The method applied to process the results of submitted games is known as the Elo rating system. Wikipedia has a decent rundown of it if you would like to know more: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Elo_rating_system. It goes through the history, the formula used, and many of the applications it has been used for over the years. This is a great place to start if you want to know how the ratings are achieved.
